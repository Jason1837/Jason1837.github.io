Skittles is a brand of fruit-flavored candy. They have a hard shell with the letter S imprinted on them. They have different fruity flavors for each color.On the inside is a chewy candy that is sweet.

Reese's Peanut Butter Cups is a candy consisting of a chocolate cup filled with peanut cream. They have a chocolate shell on the outside and soft peanut butter on the inside.
